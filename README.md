# A Day at Cream City

A browser-based workplace simulation game where your choices reveal how you think and work.

---

## What It Is

You play as Alex, a new hire three weeks into a job at a startup. Throughout the day you run into your teammates and face real workplace situations. How you respond shapes your day and generates a personality result at the end.

Every playthrough is different — the game randomly picks 6 scenes from a pool of 12.

---

## How to Run It

No installs. No setup.

```
open index.html
```

Or drag index.html into any modern browser.

---

## Files

```
index.html    The entry point
App.js        The entire game
styles.css    All the styling
```

---

## How It Works

Three chapters. Two random scenes each. Four choices per scene. One result at the end.

Your choices quietly track two things — how emotionally aware and how analytically sharp your decisions are. At the end those scores generate one of five personality archetypes with a full breakdown of your working style.

---

## Built With

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks. No image files. No external dependencies.
